## Why This Material Exists

Forward Deployed Engineer interviews are different from normal software engineering interviews.

You are not only tested on coding.

You may also be tested on:

- Understanding unclear customer problems
- Debugging production issues
- Working with messy customer data
- Designing practical systems under constraints
- Communicating trade-offs clearly
- Handling customer pressure
- Deploying AI features into real environments
- Turning vague requirements into working solutions

Most candidates prepare with generic LeetCode and system design material.

That is useful, but not enough.

FDE interviews often require a different kind of thinking.

This material is designed to help you practice that thinking.

# Suggested Portfolio Projects

To strengthen your FDE profile, consider building projects like:

## AI Customer Support Assistant

A system that reads customer support tickets, classifies issues, suggests replies, and escalates high-risk cases.

## CRM Sync Debugging Project

A backend service that syncs records from one system to another, with validation, retries, error handling, and monitoring.

## Enterprise RAG Assistant

A document assistant that retrieves answers from internal documents with citations, evaluation, and permission awareness.

## AI Deployment Readiness Dashboard

A dashboard that tracks model quality, latency, cost, retrieval quality, and failure cases.

## Workflow Automation Tool

A tool that automates a messy business workflow and includes customer discovery, integration, and production monitoring.
