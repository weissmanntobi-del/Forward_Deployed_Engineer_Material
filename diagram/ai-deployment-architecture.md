# AI Deployment Readiness Checklist

A practical checklist for engineers preparing for **Forward Deployed Engineer**, **Forward Deployed AI Engineer**, **AI Solutions Engineer**, or **customer-facing AI engineering** roles.

Building an AI demo is easy.

Deploying AI into a real customer environment is much harder.

In a demo, the data is clean, the workflow is controlled, and the users are friendly.

In production, the data is messy, users behave unpredictably, latency matters, cost matters, security matters, and the customer expects the system to work reliably.

This checklist helps you think like a Forward Deployed AI Engineer before deploying an AI system into production.

---

## Why AI Deployment Readiness Matters

Many AI projects fail not because the model is bad, but because the surrounding system is not production-ready.

Common problems include:

- Poor data quality
- Missing evaluation process
- Weak retrieval quality
- No monitoring
- No fallback behavior
- High latency
- Unexpected cost
- Security and compliance concerns
- Poor user experience
- No clear success metric
- No ownership after deployment

A Forward Deployed Engineer must think beyond the model.

You are responsible for understanding the customer problem, validating the workflow, managing risk, and making sure the AI system creates real business value.
