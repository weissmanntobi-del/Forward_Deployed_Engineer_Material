## Free FDE Interview Questions

Here are 10 realistic FDE-style interview questions.

## 1. Production Integration Failure

A customer says your integration worked in the sandbox but fails in production.

How do you debug it?

## 2. Messy Customer Data

The customer wants an AI feature, but their data is incomplete, duplicated, and inconsistent.

How do you still deliver value?

## 3. AI Feature Hallucination

Your AI assistant gives incorrect answers for some customer queries.

How do you evaluate and improve the system?

## 4. High-Pressure Customer Demo

A feature breaks during a live customer demo.

What do you say, and what do you do next?

## 5. Vague Requirement

The customer says:

“We want AI to automate our operations.”

How do you turn that into a concrete technical plan?

## 6. System Design Under Constraints

Design a system that ingests customer documents, extracts structured data, and syncs it into their internal workflow tool.

What architecture would you propose?

## 7. API Rate Limits

Your system is failing because the customer’s third-party API has strict rate limits.

How do you redesign the integration?

## 8. Customer Communication

Engineering says the proper fix takes three weeks, but the customer needs something in three days.

How do you handle this?

## 9. RAG Deployment Problem

A customer-facing RAG system retrieves irrelevant documents.

How do you debug retrieval quality?

## 10. Enterprise Deployment

The customer cannot send data to your cloud environment because of compliance restrictions.

How would you deploy or adapt the solution?
